# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its Code of Conduct.

Full text: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Our pledge (summary)

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for
everyone. Maintainers and participants are expected to be respectful and
professional in all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions).

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported privately to the maintainers via a
[GitHub Security Advisory](https://github.com/ECgear/wisediff/security/advisories/new)
or by opening a confidential channel through the project's GitHub page.
All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers may take
any action they deem appropriate, up to and including a temporary or permanent
ban from project spaces.

For the complete standards and enforcement guidelines, see the linked
Contributor Covenant 2.1 above.
